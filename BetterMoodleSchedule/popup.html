BetterMoodleSchedule

Thank you for choosing this Chrome-extension.

Functionalities

  • Uncheck the checkboxes in the Courses-table to hide course events from the schedule
  • Double click a course event to hide it from the schedule
  • Click either the day-of-the-week or the date to show hidden courses for that day again
  • Click on an event's time to customize the time of the event
  • Right click on an event's time to restore it to it's original value
